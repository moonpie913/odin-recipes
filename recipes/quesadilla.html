Quesadilla

This chicken quesadilla recipe is great to make for parties. Zesty chicken, cooked peppers, and melted cheese are a delightful combination. Cut into wedges and serve with sour cream and salsa.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Seasonings
  • Vegetables
  • Tortillas
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Season and cook the chicken
  2. Cook the vegetables with the chicken
  3. Layer mixture onto half of the tortilla
  4. Top mixture with cheese
  5. Fold tortilla and bake until cheese is melted
  6. Cut quesadilla into wedges and serve
